## 2. What We Collect

**Account Information**
- Name, email address, and profile information provided during sign-up (via Clerk)

**Educational Data**
- Project submissions, worksheet answers, checkpoint responses
- Progress records through the 10-stage method

**Usage Data**
- Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps)
- Feature usage for improving the Platform

**Parent/Guardian Data**
- Name and email for consent and communication purposes (where applicable for students under 18)

We do **not** collect:
- Payment information (we do not currently charge fees)
- Sensitive categories of personal data (health, biometric, etc.)

- To provide the Platform's educational features
- To communicate program updates and feedback
- To send parent/guardian progress updates (with consent)
- To improve the Platform based on usage patterns
- To comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your data to third parties.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on:
- **Supabase** (PostgreSQL database, hosted on AWS)
- **Vercel** (application hosting and edge functions)
- **Vercel Blob** (file storage)

We implement reasonable security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure; we encourage users to use strong passwords and report suspected breaches immediately.

---

## 5. Minors (Under 18)

We take the privacy of minors seriously. Students under 18 require parental consent before using the Platform. We limit data collection for minors to what is necessary for educational use. Parents may request to review, correct, or delete their child's data at any time.
